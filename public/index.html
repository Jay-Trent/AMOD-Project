Sales Per Department

With almost 1000k recordings, produce appears to be the most popular category overall, followed by dairy, eggs, and snacks, which each have an average of 500k entries. It's noteworthy to notice that over 80% of the total number of ordered items fall under the category "Produce, Dairy, Eggs, Snacks, Beverages, Frozen, Pantry and Bakery."

Sales By Day

The busiest days for Instacart shoppers are Sunday and Monday, when close to 1.5 million goods are ordered. Over 70% of the total items were ordered between 8 and 4 in the daytime. With the use of this data, we can time our sales and push notifications for the day's busiest times. In order to enhance sales, this would optimise the conversion of marketing efforts done through the application

Sales Per Aisle

When we focus on a single product, bananas are the top seller, accounting for more than a third of the total records from the Top 10 Best Selling Products, followed by organic strawberries and organic spinach. If we zoom in on the sales by aisle, fruits and vegetables make up more than 50% of the orders.

Insight of Department

Produce appears to be the most popular category overall with approximately 1 million recordings, followed by snacks, dairy, and eggs, which each have an average of 500 thousand entries. The fact that more than 80% of the total number of ordered items fall into the "Produce, Dairy, Eggs, Snacks, Drinks, Frozen, Pantry and Bakery" category is remarkable.

Order Frequency

The majority of customers choose to place orders either weekly or monthly. It is important to remember that 1/3 percent of clients placed more than 16 orders, and some customers had 75 or more. 15% of the client shop frequently, sometimes even once a week or less.
